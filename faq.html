Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some common questions about Brew & Bloom.

Do you offer dairy alternatives?

Yes! We offer oat milk, almond milk, and soy milk for all espresso drinks.

Do you sell whole coffee beans?

Yes. Our beans are available in-store and online so you can brew Brew & Bloom coffee at home.

Do you have seasonal drinks?

Yes! Our menu changes throughout the year with limited seasonal drinks and specialty creations.

Do you offer Wi-Fi?

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available for all guests in our café locations.

Can I order online?

Yes. You can place pickup or delivery orders through our online ordering partners.
